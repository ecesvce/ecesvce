ABOUT

Have you ever wanted to build your own robot? If you have, RAIC-SVCE is the right place for you! The Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Club is an active platform for the students to explore new trends in the industry and to network with seniors. This student-led club has a team of Robotics nerds and AI enthusiasts who teach Basic Electronics, Python programming, AI, Robotics and various cool stuffs to make engineering fun. The club offers vital guidance to build projects and also organises competitions for the students to showcase their talents.

'Of the students, By the students, For the students.'
