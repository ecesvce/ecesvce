ECE Association organized a webinar on ‘Image Processing for Machine Vision Applications’ delivered by Dr.R. Vani, Professor ECE Department, SRM Institute of Technology.

TECHNXT

3rd – 7th June 2020

TechNXT was a 5-day National Level technical fest conducted by the ECE Department in association with the Institute of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering student chapter (IETE-SF). The event consisted of 4 Webinar sessions and a Paper Presentation contest.

QUARANTBUSTER!

5th - 10th May 2020

A five day, one of a kind online event - ‘QUARANTBUSTER!’ was conducted by ECEA, IETE - SF & RAIC during the lockdown period. 24 teams with a total of 62 participants, participated from their homes, with Mr.Balabharathi D., Embedded Consultant presiding over the event as Chief Guest and Judge for the event. The problem statements were given by the industry expert and chosen by the teams.

PYTHON PROGRAMMING

10th – 14th March 2020

The Python programming workshop was a 5-day (15 hours) certification course conducted by the RAIC under IETE-SF. A total of 38, 2nd and the 3rd year ECE department students attended the session. All the sessions were hands-on sessions handled by the 3rd year RAIC mentors.

R PROGRAMMING

11th - 15th February 2020

The R-programming workshop was a 5-day certification course conducted by ECEA, IETE & RAIC. A total of 50 participants comprising of the 2nd and the 3rd year of the ECE department attended the session. The first four sessions were handled by the faculty members of CSE & IT departments and the final session was a hands-on session conducted in the placement cell by Dr. Anand Lakshmanan, Founder and CEO of Sirpi Products and Services Pvt Ltd.

MAKE-A-THON 2.0

7th Feburary 2020

The 24 hour Make-A-Thon conducted by ECEA, IETE-SF and RAIC on 7th and 8th February 2020. The Inter Department hardware and software event was conducted, where over 40 teams sent in their abstracts where only 24 teams were shortlisted. Winners were selected by Mr.Umamahesh and Mr.Akash Jain.
