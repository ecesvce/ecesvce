EVENTS

LINE FOLLOWER PROGRAMMING EVENT

Robotics and Artificial Intelligence Club conducted the 'COMPLEX LINE FOLLOWER PROGRAMMING EVENT' exclusively for the RAIC members from 11th August to 15th August, 2020. The members participated as teams of four and were guided by their respective mentors. The winners and runners were announced on 23rd August, 2020 through Slack.

UNBOXING 1.0

RAIC conducted ‘UNBOXING 1.0’ on 11th September 2020. It was presented by Mr.Sudesh Kumar S (Senior Mentor and President of RAIC), Final year ECE and was hosted through the online platform ‘ZOOM’. The speaker unboxed the project which constituted of the automation of his room with the help of microcontrollers and sensors. He demystified the project and explained about the components, connections and how it was set up.
